MACEDONIAN ARTS COUNCIL

Dedicates itself to the promotion and affirmation of Macedonian cultural heritage. Our main objective is to introduce to people worldwide the rich traditions, language, literature, and art of a nation whose diverse cultural threads are interwoven into the ethnic tapestries of the world.
